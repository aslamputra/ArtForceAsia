About Us

PT Artforceasia is an emerging company in the art and antique trading industry, founded just two years ago. Despite being in the early stages of development, we have established a solid foundation with the ambition to become a leading player in the market. Over the past two years, our focus has been on building a robust network, crafting effective marketing strategies, and conducting in-depth research on both the local and international art and antique markets.

VISI

To promote paintings by the younger generation of Indonesian artists, PT Artforceasia strives to provide a platform that supports and introduces emerging talents. We believe that young artists possess unique creativity and perspectives that deserve recognition. Through exhibitions, online galleries, and collaborations with art communities, we aim to showcase their work to broader markets, both locally and internationally.

MISSION

To make paintings more accessible to all, PT Artforceasia is committed to presenting art that everyone can enjoy. We aim to bridge the gap between artists and the wider public by providing a platform that allows artwork to be viewed, appreciated, and owned by people from all walks of life, through both physical exhibitions and online galleries.

Our Story

PT Artforceasia was founded with a mission to revitalize the value of art and history in modern life by focusing on the trade of paintings and antiques. Understanding that art and antiques carry not only aesthetic value but also high investment potential, we are committed to providing a curated selection of the finest, authentic works of art and antique items.
